# Josh's Vision: AI for the Electrical Business

## The Personal Agent

Every team member gets a personal AI agent they interact with primarily through voice. The agent acts as a working companion that:

- Captures job details, phone calls, and decisions in real time (initiated by the user, not passive surveillance)
- Manages schedules, priorities, and prompts you throughout the day to stay on track
- Answers questions on the spot: fault-finding guidance, product lookups, regulations, and drawing on your own job history to suggest solutions

## The Business Brain

The personal agents feed into a shared business intelligence layer (with explicit consent) that can:

- Search across accounting, invoicing, and job management systems (Laser Pro, Simpro, etc.)
- Flag anomalies: price increases from suppliers, jobs running below target gross profit margins, overbought materials
- Monitor operational essentials: vehicle fleet servicing, compliance dates, and renewals
- Send routine communications (service reminders, booking confirmations) autonomously for pre-approved categories

## The Hive Mind (Future State)

Over time, agents across the team begin to cross-reference each other's knowledge, surfacing connections like "a colleague solved a similar problem on a past job." This builds a living institutional memory that compounds over years.

## The Why

The end goal isn't just efficiency for profit's sake. By tightening waste and automating the administrative burden, the business can:

- Lower rates for customers without sacrificing margins
- Free tradespeople to focus on quality work and face-to-face relationships
- Operate to a defined income target, then redirect surplus toward social good and community outcomes

**Design principles:** Human-initiated input. Proactive output. Operator-controlled privacy. Voice-first interaction. Build trust through stepping stones, not big bangs.
